Compound Interest

This application allows you to calculate your monthly mortgage based on the principal you have to pay to buy your home. This application takes input from user to calculate the mortgage. This application asks user to input principal, interest rate and the number of years the user would take to pay the total mortgage. It also shows the remaining mortagae amount after each payment of the mortgage. It also allows the user to see the their payment chart by plotting a line chart of their payment and remaining balance of their mortagae.
